rice and sausage dish

Discription

this dish is easy and tasty, its our goto for a quick meal (usally lunch!)

Ingredients

  • 1 pound jimmy dean sausage
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 onion
  • small onion bits for decoration

Steps

  1. cook 2 cups of Rice
  2. chop onion into small pieces
  3. brown sausage and onion together
  4. mix together and sprinkle onion bits on top
  5. enjoy!
