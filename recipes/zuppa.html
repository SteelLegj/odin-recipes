Zuppa

Description

zuppa is a fantastic soup to have in your goto list. its flavorful, fullbodied, and easy to modify to fit the needs of your dietery needs.

Ingredients

  • 11 cups of water
  • kale, about 8 leafs
  • 8 red potatos
  • 5 chicken bullion cubes
  • garlic paste 2 Table Spoons
  • 1 pound Jimmy Dean Regular sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • french bread

Steps

  1. boil water in large pot
  2. peel potatos and cut in half, then make thin slices
  3. add potatos, garlic paste and bullion cubes to pot and start a 30 minite timer
  4. cut onion in small pieces
  5. brwon sausage and onions together in a pan
  6. take leafs off the stem of the kale and add to pot
  7. add sausage and onions to pot once fully cooked
  8. when the 30 minute timer goes off, add heavy whipping cream and set the timer for 10 minutes
  9. when timer goes off remove from heat and enjoy!
